## What does it actually do? (Features)

    Real-Time Chat: Powered by Socket.io, because waiting 2 seconds for a reply feels like an eternity in Indian IST (Indian Stretchable Time).

    One-to-One Chat: For when you want to gossip about your manager without them knowing.

    Group Chats: For when you want to plan a trip to Goa that will inevitably be cancelled 2 hours before the flight.

    User Authentication: Using JWT. Because security matters, and I don't want your "Sharma Ji ka Beta" reading your private texts.

    Search Users: Find your friends, or that one person you met at a hackathon whose name you kind of remember.
